Purpose of the Lulu AI

The purpose of the MLL AI is to assist students providing interactive, dynamic language support. The AI should adapt to the student’s proficiency level and help them navigate language learning challenges in everyday school situations.

Services

Meet Lulu, a generative AI chatbot that's here to help young people in Luxembourg learn new languages! In a country where a mix of languages is the norm, Lulu has become the perfect partner for anyone looking to master French, German, Luxembourgish, and even English, all in a light and fun way. With a relaxed approach, Lulu turns every chat into an interactive lesson, packed with tips, exercises, and even games. This way, young people can practice while having fun, exploring the world of languages and feeling more confident in their day-to-day lives in such a multicultural environment. With Lulu, learning a new language becomes an exciting adventure!
